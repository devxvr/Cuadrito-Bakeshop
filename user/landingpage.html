CUADRITO BAKESHOP

Cuadrito Bakeshop has been a beloved fixture in Zamboanga City for the past 25 years. Originally founded by a passionate baker, the shop has grown from its humble beginnings on Gov. Alvarez St. into a trusted name in the community, known for its fresh and delicious cakes and pastries. The business has been passed down through the family, with the next generation now continuing the tradition of baking with love and care.

While the shop thrived on word-of-mouth for many years, Cuadrito Bakeshop is now stepping into the digital world. With the launch of its online ordering system, customers can easily order their favorite treats for any occasion, bringing the shop’s handcrafted goodness right to their doorstep.
